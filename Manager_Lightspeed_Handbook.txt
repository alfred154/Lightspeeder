Inventory Adjustments (Broken, stolen, or expired)

If something is broken, stolen, used, or expires you must remove it from your store’s inventory. 

Step-by-Step:
1. From the Sell screen, tap the menu icon (☰) in the upper left
2. Select Dashboard
3. On the Dashboard screen, tap the menu icon again (upper left)
4. Navigate to Catalog > Products
5. Search for the product that is broken, stolen, used, or expired 
6. Touch the arrow to the left of the product to show a dropdown
7. Touch “Adjust inventory”
8. Choose the store where the product must be adjusted
9. Change the “Adjustment reason” to match why it’s being removed 

from inventory

10. Mark how many need to be removed
11. Press “Adjust inventory” and the action will be complete.

Dejavoo Error” Payment – Proper Use Guide
Occasionally, a customer may tap or insert their card too quickly.
 In these cases, the terminal (card reader) processes the payment, 
but Lightspeed remains stuck on the payment screen.
If that happens:
*Do not guess. Do not hit random buttons.*
Incorrect actions can result in *double charges* or *lost revenue* for the store.

Follow These Steps Exactly
1. Print the small receipt from the Dejavoo terminal.
This confirms the customer was actually charged.
If you skip this, there’s no proof of payment and 
the product  could walk out unpaid.
2. Only after confirming payment, select “Dejavoo Error” 
in Lightspeed to complete the transaction.
3. After the sale is finalized:
    📸 Take a photo of:
     • The *Lightspeed receipt*, and
     • The *Dejavoo receipt*

4. Post both images in your store’s Discord channel.
Tag *@accounting* and *@lightspeed* for reporting.

🚨 This is not optional.
Skipping any of these steps can result in loss and will lead 
to disciplinary action.
Thanks for handling this correctly and keeping operations 
smooth.
